Teriyaki Pork Chops

Description

Easy teriyaki flavored pork chops.

Ingredients

Ingredients you will need:

  • 3/4 cup teriyaki sauce.
  • 2 & 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt.
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper.
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  • ~4 pork chops.

Steps

  1. Combine teriyaki sauce, garlic salt, and black pepper into a bowl.
  2. Place pork chops into a bag and pour 3/4 of the teriyaki sauce mixture into the bag. (You will use the remaining 1/4 of the sauce later)
  3. Let pork chops marinate for 30 minuets.
  4. Once the pork chops have marinated, place skillet on medium heat and add olive oil.
  5. Take the pork chops out of the marinade and place them in the skillet. (Discard used marinade)
  6. Cover and cook pork chops till they have browned. ~15 minuets
  7. Flip and continue cooking till they are cooked through.
  8. Once the pork chops are done, add the remaining 1/4 of teriyaki mixture to the skillet.
  9. Cover skillet and let simmer for about 5 minutes.
  10. Enjoy.

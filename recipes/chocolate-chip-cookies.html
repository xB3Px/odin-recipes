Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies from scratch. Recipe is from Clifford Stoll's book: Cuckoo's Egg.

Ingredients

Ingredients you will need:

  • 2 eggs.
  • 1 cup brown sugar.
  • 1/2 cup sugar.
  • 2 sticks of softened butter.
  • 2 & 1/4 cup flour.
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda.
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla.
  • 3 tablespoon cocoa. (optional)
  • 2 cups chocolate chips.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. Mix eggs, brown sugar, regular sugar, and butter in a mixing bowl.
  3. Fold in remaining ingredients.
  4. Place blobs of batter on cookie sheet.
  5. Bake for 10 minuets.
  6. Let cool once finished.
  7. Enjoy.

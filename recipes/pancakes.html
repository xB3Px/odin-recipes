Pancakes

Description

Quick and simple homemade pancakes using common kitchen ingredients.

Ingredients

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1 cup flour.
  • 1 teaspoon ugar.
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  • 3 teaspoon baking powder.
  • 1 egg.
  • 3/4 cup milk.
  • 2 teaspoon cooking oil.
  • Pat of butter for pan.

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients expect butter to a mxixng bowl.
  2. Mix ingredients until uniformly blended.
  3. Place cooking pan on medium heat.
  4. Add pat of butter and melt across surface of pan
  5. Add pancake batter until its about 4 inchs in diameter. Make sure pancakes do not touch.
  6. Flip once top of pancake batter starts to bubble.
  7. Wait about 30 seconds to a minute to flip again.
  8. Continue cooking till desired browness.
  9. Repeat steps 4 - 8 till all the batter is used up.
  10. Enjoy.

Pancakes

Description

Quick and simple homemade pancakes using common kitchen ingredients.

Ingredients

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1 cup flour.
  • 1 teaspoon sugar.
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  • 3 teaspoon baking powder.
  • 1 egg.
  • 3/4 cup milk.
  • 2 teaspoon cooking oil.
  • *3 or 4 pats of butter for pan.

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients except butter into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mix ingredients thoroughly.
  3. Place cooking pan on medium heat.
  4. Add 1 pat of butter and melt across surface of pan.
  5. Add several 4 inch diameter blobs of pancake batter. (Make sure pancakes do not touch)
  6. Flip once top of pancakes start to bubble.
  7. Wait about 30 seconds to a minute to flip again.
  8. Continue cooking till desired browness.
  9. Repeat steps 4 - 9 till all the batter is used up.
  10. Enjoy.

Banana Bread

Description

Banana Bread made easy.

Ingredients

Ingredients and items you will need:

  • 2-3 ripe mashed bananas.
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda.
  • 1 pinch salt.
  • 3/4 cup sugar.
  • 1 large egg.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  • 1 1/2 cup flour.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Add all ingredients into a mixing bowl.
  3. Mix ingredients thoroughly.
  4. Fill bread/loaf pan halfway with batter.
  5. Place in oven for 35-40 minuets.
  6. Let cool once finished.
  7. Enjoy.
